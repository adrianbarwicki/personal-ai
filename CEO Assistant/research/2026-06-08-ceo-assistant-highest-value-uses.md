## Question

What is the single biggest productivity or strategic gain Adrian can extract from the CEO assistant n8n workflow — and which use patterns unlock it?

## Why it matters

The workflow is live but its value depends entirely on how consistently and creatively Adrian uses it. Before investing more time in extending the agent, it's worth understanding which category of use (idea capture, task triage, engineering backlog, decisions) produces the highest return — so that energy goes to the highest-leverage habit first.

## How to research

- [ ] Review the first 2–4 weeks of filed artifacts: which category appears most often, and which ones actually moved the needle (led to a decision, unblocked an engineer, cleared Adrian's head)?
- [ ] Identify the friction points in Adrian's current workflow that the assistant *could* absorb but doesn't yet — e.g. meeting notes → action items, recurring weekly todos, Slack threads that die without a decision.
- [ ] Look at comparable "second brain / CEO inbox" setups (e.g. how operators at Sonder, Vacasa, or indie PMS founders use AI capture tools) for patterns worth borrowing.
- [ ] Map the top 3 candidate gains (e.g. "never lose an idea again", "engineering backlog always fresh", "decision log for accountability") against effort-to-sustain and estimate rough weekly time saved per gain.
- [ ] Validate the top candidate with a 1-week intentional test: route every qualifying thought through the assistant and see what changes.
