## 1. Executive Summary

Enhanced Rentals GmbH ("ER GmbH" or "the Client"), a German limited liability company registered at Amtsgericht Mannheim (HRB 753079), has engaged Gamma Global Partners FZCO ("Gamma" or "the Consultant"), a UAE-based free zone company, to provide sales support, technology integration, and regional representation services in the GCC region and globally.

Under this arrangement, Gamma employs or contracts sales representatives on behalf of ER GmbH and reinvoices the associated personnel costs to ER GmbH at cost plus a 10% service margin. This document establishes that the applied pricing methodology is consistent with the arm's length principle as required under German tax law (§1 AStG) and the OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines.

## 3. Description of the Controlled Transaction

### 3.1 Nature of Services

Gamma provides the following categories of services to ER GmbH:

**Sales Support**
Identifying, approaching, and engaging potential clients and partners; presenting ER GmbH's software platform; supporting commercial discussions and negotiations with prospective customers.

**Integration and Technical Support**
Assisting customers with onboarding, implementation, and deployment of ER GmbH's software platform; coordinating technical integrations with ER GmbH's backend systems.

**Account Management**
Maintaining ongoing relationships with regional customers; supporting platform adoption and operational continuity.

**Regional Representation**
Representing ER GmbH in the GCC market; supporting market expansion and partnership development.

**Personnel Provision**
Hiring, contracting, and managing one or more sales representatives located in the GCC region to perform the above services. Gamma remains the legal employer and bears all employment, tax, statutory, and contractual obligations with respect to these representatives.

### 3.2 Flow of Services and Costs

```
ER GmbH (Germany)
     │
     │  Pays monthly service fee per representative
     │  (Cost + 10% markup = 5,750 AED/rep/month)
     ▼
Gamma Global Partners FZCO (Dubai)
     │
     │  Employs/contracts Sales Representatives
     │  Bears all employment costs and obligations
     ▼
GCC Sales Representatives (UAE / GCC region)
     │
     └─ Perform services for ER GmbH's benefit in GCC market
```

### 3.3 Compensation Structure

The compensation mechanism agreed between the parties is as follows:

- Gamma identifies and employs sales representatives for ER GmbH's benefit in the GCC region.
- All direct and indirect employment costs (salary, benefits, statutory contributions, overhead allocation) are borne initially by Gamma.
- Gamma invoices ER GmbH monthly on a **cost-plus basis**: actual costs incurred per representative plus a **10% service markup**.
- The contractually agreed monthly fee is **AED 5,750 per representative per month**, reflecting this cost-plus-10% arrangement.
- Invoices are issued monthly; payment is due within 15 days of receipt.
- The number of assigned representatives can be adjusted with 30 days' written notice.

### 3.4 Scope of Arrangement

The arrangement primarily covers the GCC region but may extend to global customers where Gamma supports ER GmbH's commercial activities. The agreement does not grant exclusivity.

## 5. Transfer Pricing Method

### 5.1 Method Selected: Cost Plus Method (Kostenaufschlagsmethode)

The **Cost Plus Method** (CPM) has been selected as the most appropriate transfer pricing method for this arrangement. The CPM is prescribed by OECD Guidelines (Chapter II, §2.39–2.55) and recognized under §1 AStG as appropriate for service transactions where a service provider incurs costs and charges those costs to a related party at a markup.

### 5.2 Rationale for Method Selection

The Cost Plus Method is the most appropriate method for the following reasons:

- Gamma is providing defined, routine services (personnel provision, sales support, integration) with limited entrepreneurial risk on the service delivery itself.
- The primary costs are personnel-related and quantifiable.
- Gamma does not bear platform development risk or customer credit risk.
- This method is widely applied by tax authorities and consistent with OECD guidance for contract service providers.
- Comparable uncontrolled prices are not readily available for this type of regional personnel provision arrangement.

### 5.3 Cost Base

The cost base for the markup includes all direct and reasonably allocable indirect costs borne by Gamma in connection with each representative, including:

- Representative salary or contractor fee
- Mandatory UAE social/statutory contributions (where applicable)
- Relevant overhead allocation (management, HR administration, office, communications)
- Any other directly attributable employment-related costs

**Note:** ER GmbH should obtain periodic cost breakdowns from Gamma to confirm the cost base underpinning the AED 5,750/month figure remains consistent with the cost-plus-10% formula.

### 5.4 Markup Applied

The agreed markup is **10% on the cost base**. This is an internal group policy for this relationship.

### 5.5 Arm's Length Range Assessment

A 10% markup on cost for a routine service provider performing defined sales support, personnel provision, and integration services is consistent with:

- OECD guidance that routine service providers typically earn markups in the range of 5%–15% depending on complexity and risk assumed.
- Published benchmarking studies for staffing and sales support service companies in the MENA and global markets generally indicate arm's length markups in the 5%–15% range for comparable functions.
- German tax authority (Betriebsprüfung) practice, which generally accepts cost-plus markups in the 5%–15% range for low-risk service transactions.

**Conclusion:** The 10% markup falls within the arm's length range and is considered defensible under German and international transfer pricing standards.
