## 1. Overview

This document describes the commercial and operational relationship between Enhanced Rentals GmbH ("ER GmbH") and Gamma Global Partners FZCO ("Gamma"). It is intended to provide a clear, factual account of the nature, structure, rationale, and terms of the intercompany arrangement for use in transfer pricing documentation, internal governance, regulatory enquiries, and tax compliance.

---

## 2. The Entities

### Enhanced Rentals GmbH

Enhanced Rentals GmbH is a German limited liability company (GmbH) registered at Amtsgericht Mannheim under HRB 753079, with its registered office at Daimlerstr. 34, 69190 Walldorf, Germany. ER GmbH develops and operates a software platform for the rental industry. The company holds all intellectual property rights to the platform and is responsible for its development, maintenance, and licensing.

ER GmbH seeks to expand its software platform into international markets, including the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region, and requires a local operational and commercial presence in those markets to support sales and customer onboarding activities.

### Gamma Global Partners FZCO

Gamma Global Partners FZCO is a UAE-based free zone company (FZCO) registered under Trade License No. 56562, with its registered office at IFZA Properties, Building A1, Dubai Digital Park, Dubai Silicon Oasis, Dubai, UAE. Gamma operates as a technology sales, integration, and consulting firm active in the GCC region and globally.

Gamma possesses local market knowledge, an established GCC business network, UAE regulatory infrastructure (trade license, corporate banking, employment framework), and the operational capacity to hire and manage local personnel on behalf of international technology companies.

---

## 3. Strategic Rationale for the Relationship

ER GmbH's expansion into the GCC market requires a physically present, commercially capable partner that can:

- Identify and engage prospective customers in the GCC region.
- Represent ER GmbH's software platform to regional businesses.
- Hire and manage locally-based sales and account representatives.
- Support technical onboarding and integration work with regional customers.
- Navigate local regulatory, employment, and commercial requirements.

Establishing a direct legal entity in the UAE would require significant time, capital, and administrative investment. Engaging Gamma as a regional integration and sales support partner provides ER GmbH with immediate market access, operational capability, and employment infrastructure at substantially lower cost and risk — while preserving ER GmbH's full ownership of all intellectual property and customer contractual relationships.

---

## 4. Nature of the Commercial Relationship

The relationship between ER GmbH and Gamma is that of **client and authorized integration/sales partner**, as formalized in the Sales Support and Integration Partner Agreement signed 01.03.2026. Key characteristics:

**Independent Contractor Relationship.** Gamma and its representatives are independent contractors. No employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship exists between the parties. Gamma bears all employment, tax, insurance, and statutory obligations in relation to its personnel.

**Non-Exclusive Partnership.** The agreement does not confer exclusivity on Gamma, unless separately agreed in writing. ER GmbH retains the right to engage other partners in the GCC region.

**Partner-of-Record Status.** When Gamma introduces a customer or business opportunity to ER GmbH, Gamma is recognized as the Partner-of-Record for that customer for 24 months from introduction. This protects Gamma's commercial interest in accounts it originates and incentivizes active prospecting.

**Non-Circumvention Protection.** ER GmbH has agreed not to bypass or circumvent Gamma with respect to customers or partners introduced by Gamma. Any commercial relationship entered into by ER GmbH with such parties within 12 months of introduction is attributed to Gamma's efforts.

**IP and Customer Ownership.** All intellectual property, including the software platform, remains exclusively owned by ER GmbH. ER GmbH retains the right to contract directly with customers for software licenses and platform subscriptions. Gamma may independently contract and invoice customers for integration, implementation, and support services — leveraging the platform under ER GmbH's authorization.

---

## 5. Services Provided by Gamma

Gamma delivers the following services to ER GmbH under the agreement:

**Sales Development.** Gamma proactively identifies, qualifies, and approaches potential customers and partners in the GCC region. It presents the ER GmbH platform, supports commercial discussions, and facilitates introductions to ER GmbH's sales and management team.

**Integration and Technical Support.** Gamma coordinates the technical onboarding of new customers, supports deployment, assists with system integrations, and ensures operational setup is completed to ER GmbH's standards.

**Account Management.** Gamma manages ongoing relationships with GCC-based accounts, responds to operational queries, and supports platform adoption and retention.

**Regional Representation.** Gamma represents ER GmbH's interests in the GCC market more broadly, pursuing partnership opportunities and contributing to ER GmbH's regional market development strategy.

**Personnel Provision.** Gamma hires, employs, and manages one or more sales and account representatives located in the GCC region. These representatives work under Gamma's supervision but serve ER GmbH's commercial objectives. Gamma is fully responsible for the representatives' employment terms, compensation, tax compliance, and administration.

---

## 6. Cost Reimbursement and Compensation

Gamma's services are compensated through a monthly cost-plus service fee mechanism:

- Gamma incurs all employment-related costs (salary, benefits, statutory contributions, overhead) for each representative it assigns to the ER GmbH engagement.
- Gamma reinvoices these costs to ER GmbH on a monthly basis, applying an internal margin of 10% over cost — reflecting Gamma's role as legal employer, administrator, and risk-bearing party.
- The contractually agreed monthly fee is **AED 5,750 per representative per month**.
- Invoices are issued monthly; payment is due within 15 days.
- The number of assigned representatives may be adjusted with 30 days' written notice by ER GmbH.

This cost-plus arrangement ensures that Gamma is appropriately compensated for its operational role without creating a profit-sharing or performance-linked dependency. It is consistent with standard employer-of-record and cost recharge practices for international business services.

---

## 7. Governance and Oversight

**Contract Governance.** The relationship is governed by the Sales Support and Integration Partner Agreement (01.03.2026). Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

**Reporting.** Gamma's representatives maintain regular communication with ER GmbH's management team regarding sales pipeline, account status, and technical progress. The frequency and format of reporting are agreed operationally.

**Non-Solicitation.** ER GmbH has agreed not to directly hire or engage Gamma's representatives during the term of the agreement and for 12 months following termination. Breach of this obligation would result in compensation equal to 6 months of the representative service fee payable to Gamma.

**Dispute Resolution.** All disputes are subject to German law and the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Karlsruhe, Germany.

---

## 8. Duration of the Relationship

The agreement commenced on 01.03.2026 and continues on a month-to-month basis. Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice. All fees accrued prior to termination remain payable.
