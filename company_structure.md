### Intra-Group Loan / Credit Facility Structure

```
Alphateam Hackers GmbH (DE)
        │
        │  Credit facility — lower interest rate
        │  Documented by: nota odsetkowa / Zinsabrechnung
        ▼
Vester Fundacja Rodzinna (PL)
        │
        │  Credit facility — higher interest rate (spread = VESTER margin)
        │  Documented by: nota odsetkowa
        ▼
RENTUJEMY Sp. z o.o. (PL)
```

**Key points:**
- Both facilities are governed by **credit facility agreements** (not single fixed-term loans) — drawdowns are flexible
- VESTER acts as an intra-group treasury vehicle, earning the **spread** between the two rates
- All interest is documented via **nota odsetkowa** (not VAT invoices) — interest is VAT-exempt
- Interest income earned by VESTER from RENTUJEMY must be declared in **JPK7M as zwolniony turnover** (VAT-exempt sales)
- Interest paid by RENTUJEMY to VESTER is a financial cost — not in the VAT register, P&L only
- **Transfer pricing documentation** required for both legs if transaction values exceed 10M PLN/year
- **Art. 15c CIT** interest limitation rules apply to RENTUJEMY (net financing costs above 3M PLN threshold)
