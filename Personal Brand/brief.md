# Adrian Barwicki — Personal Brand Brief

## Who He Is

Polish founder. UAE tax resident. Physics degree (Heidelberg) → SAP engineer → entrepreneur.

Currently:
- Co-CEO, **Enhanced Rentals GmbH** (Walldorf) — AI-powered SaaS for STR operators, Hostaway-native, €19–35/prop/mo
- CEO, **Alphateam Hackers GmbH** — the dev/IP entity
- Prezes Zarządu, **RENTUJEMY Sp. z o.o.** — ~300-property STR operation in Poland (the product's own power user)
- CEO, **Vester Fundacja Rodzinna**
- Based across: Warsaw / Walldorf / Dubai

**The core story:** Adrian ran 300 properties and built the AI tools he needed because nothing on the market was good enough. He is simultaneously the customer and the founder. This is the most authentic and powerful narrative.

**Lifestyle:** BJJ (primary identity anchor), mountaineering. Genuinely international life — not a burnout grind founder.

**In his own words (use this everywhere):**
> "I just do BJJ mainly and in recovery I try to run a STR operator in Poland with 300 properties and build AI Platform that allows to automate most of the work for the STR so I can train more BJJ"

This is the soul of the brand. The inversion (BJJ first, business second) is memorable and authentic. The circularity (automate so I can train more BJJ) is the product pitch in one sentence.

## Brand Architecture — Two Entities, One Story

| Entity | What it is | Brand role |
|---|---|---|
| **RENTUJEMY Sp. z o.o.** | The operator — ~300 STR properties in Poland & Dubai | Systems & processes credibility. The proof that the model works. |
| **Enhanced Rentals GmbH** | The SaaS platform — AI bots, hotlines, operations, owner portal | The product born from RENTUJEMY's operational experience. |

Adrian's personal brand bridges both. RENTUJEMY is the living proof. Enhanced Rentals is what you buy.

**Content sequencing — phased approach:**

**Phase 1 (now):** RENTUJEMY only. Operator systems, scaling insights, process content. Build credibility and audience as a serious STR operator. No product mentions.

**Phase 2 (once audience is established):** Introduce Enhanced Rentals as "the tool we built to run RENTUJEMY." The reveal is earned, not cold-pitched. Audience already trusts Adrian as an operator — the product becomes the natural next question.

**Never conflate the two in copy.** "We run 300 properties" = RENTUJEMY. "We built the AI platform" = Enhanced Rentals.

## Tone of Voice

- Direct, no corporate fluff
- Specific over vague (numbers, properties, countries — not "scale" and "impact")
- Confident without being a bro
- Self-deprecating where authentic (the BJJ framing is a feature, not a bug)
- Lifestyle is earned, not performed
- Polish identity visible but not limiting — international in outlook

---

## What to Avoid

- Sensitive tax topics, tax avoidance, "how to get rich" framing
- Generic founder clichés ("hustle", "game-changer", "disrupting", "revolutionise")
- Outdated narrative (adrianbarwicki.com still says "Software Engineer, Frankfurt, InsurTech" — completely stale)
