~€20,000 per feature

what i built → three pieces 06 / 08
the three things i installed across the companies:
  1. A product-wide docs repo — engineering, design, bizdev, accounting. Readable by humans AND AI. The shared brain of the company.
  2. PMs draft tickets with AI — informed by the full docs repo, not just product specs. Better tickets, faster. AI then reads the tickets in Jira and codes the task.
  3. Engineers review + coordinate — they don't type line by line. Judgement, not output. The bottleneck moves from typing speed to taste.
— not a reorg, a new operating model adrianbarwicki.com
