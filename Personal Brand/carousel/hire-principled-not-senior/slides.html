They've seen a lot of code.
They have pattern recognition
you can't fake.
02 / 07

when typing
was the bottleneck.

in 2026 can:

· Write down what an agent should build
· Recognise when output is wrong
· Ship without ego
04 / 07
That's a
principled engineer.

on any "senior" track

They're the only ones
whose output is still growing.

Principles is the right one.
