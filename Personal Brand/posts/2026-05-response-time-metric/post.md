## Final copy (paste-ready)

> Your apartment isn't the problem. Your reply time is.
>
> I run ~300 STR properties. We looked at the data across every reservation. The single biggest predictor of a 5-star review isn't the apartment, the linens, or the coffee machine.
>
> It's how fast you reply when something goes wrong.
>
> – Sub-10-minute reply on a real issue → 5 stars
> – 2-hour reply on a perfect apartment → 4 stars
>
> Guests don't expect perfection. They expect to feel responded to.
>
> Most operators obsess over the property. New pillows. Better lighting. Premium bath products. None of it moves the needle.
>
> The thing that moves the needle is the thing they're not measuring: response time at 11pm on a Tuesday.
>
> Your apartment is probably fine. Your response time isn't.
