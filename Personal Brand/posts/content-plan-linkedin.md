## Format rules across all posts

- **Hook in the first line** — LinkedIn truncates after ~3 lines. The hook must work alone.
- **No emojis in the hook.** Earn the right to use one later in the post if at all.
- **Line breaks every 1–2 sentences.** Mobile readability > paragraph density.
- **One claim per post.** Don't try to make two contrarian arguments in one post.
- **No hashtag stuffing.** Max 2. Ideally 0.
- **Comment-prime within 5 minutes.** Drop the "save the inevitable thread" caveat as the first comment yourself. Earns engagement signal + heads off bad-faith replies.
- **Don't link out in the post body.** Drop links in the first comment. LinkedIn down-ranks posts with external links.
